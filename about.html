About Endless Dodger

I built a endless game playable in browser where users dodge the objects and earn scores as my first project as beginner..

How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or A/D to move left and right
  • Avoid the falling blocks
  • Survive and increase your score
  • The game gets harder as your score increases

Game Features

  • Smooth controls
  • High score tracking
  • Animated background effect
