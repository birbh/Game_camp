About Endless Dodger

Endless Dodger is a fast-paced arcade game where you control a player trying to avoid falling obstacles.

How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or A/D to move left and right
  • Avoid the falling orange blocks
  • Survive as long as possible to increase your score
  • The game gets progressively harder as your score increases

Game Features

  • Smooth controls and responsive gameplay
  • Progressive difficulty scaling
  • High score tracking
  • Animated background effects
